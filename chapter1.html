This chapter explores the composition of light. Usually we will be talking about the properties of light emitted by lasers. Through the chapters we will use a visual models like the one shown to the right to represent a laser beam.

Light carries energy and this energy comes in discrete packets called photons. The number carried in even a fairly weak beam, as in a small laser pointer, is truly stupendous. Put your hand in a weak laser beam and a every second about 1,000,000,000,000 (a thousand trillion) photons will be htiting it.
